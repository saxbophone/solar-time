Solar Time by saxbophone

This simple web app will show you the precise solar time of your location, using HTML5 Geolocation.

To see the local solar time for your location, click allow when prompted to allow this page access to your location. This is to calculate what the true solar time of your location is to nearest second based on your longitude position on the globe.
